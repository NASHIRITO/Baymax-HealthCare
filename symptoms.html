ASTHMA

symtopms of asthama:

  • Shortness of breath occurs like a fit
  • Exhaling air, in particular, is difficult
  • Wheezing or humming breathing noises.
  • Frequent cough, especially at night

BRAIN TUMOUR

symptoms of Brain Tumour:

  • Severe, persistent headaches
  • Seizures (fits)
  • Persistent nausea, vomiting and drowsiness
  • Mental or behavioural changes, such as memory problems

DEPRESSION

symptoms of Depression:

  • Loss of interest or pleasure in liked activities
  • Feeling low self-esteem or Guilt
  • Sleeping too much or very less
  • Eating too little or too much

DIABETES

symptoms of Diabetes:

  • Increased thirst
  • Slow-healing sores
  • Frequent infections, such as gums or skin infections
  • Blurred vision

FLU

symptoms of Flu:

  • Fever or feeling feverish/chills
  • Sore throat
  • Cough,Runny Nose.
  • Headaches,Muscle or Body aches.

KIDNEY STONE

symptoms provided by kidney stone

  • Pain in the groin and/or side
  • White blood cells or pus in the urine
  • Reduced amount of excreted urine excreted
  • Burning sensation during urination

MALARIA

symptoms of Malaria:

  • Fever
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Rapid breathing,Rapid heart rate
  • Nausea and vomiting

TUBERCULOSIS

symptoms of TB:

  • Coughing for three or more weeks.
  • Coughing up blood or mucus.
  • Unintentional weight loss.
  • Chest pain, or pain with breathing
